Permafrost

Summary.

Students learn what Permafrost is and the consequences of Permafrost thaw due to climate change. In the Jupyter Notebook, students calculate the heat flux through permafrost and increase computational skills through working in Python with real-world data. Developed by Penny Rowe and Steven Neshyba.

 

 

Materials

Common Core Standards and Learning Goals

  • Increase climate literacy
  • Gain computational skills through performing calculations in Excel.

These materials were developed for a college-level Physics class, and need to be modified for the High School level.
